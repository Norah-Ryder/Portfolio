Contextual Drift in LLMs

Ten real conversations across ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, and Claude, documented end to end: how models construct a role-frame early in sustained interaction and reason from inside it, how verifiable content carries unverifiable cargo, and what varies — measurably — between labs and between versions.

You don’t have to trust my reading. Every quoted line links to its span in the redacted source transcript, and the transcripts are published. The walkthroughs are the analysis; the exhibits are the evidence; the method page states what this design can and cannot support, including the numbers that don’t flatter it.
